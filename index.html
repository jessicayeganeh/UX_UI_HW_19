My UX Skills

Human-Centered Design

Human-centered design is a necessity when it comes to UX. Approaching a problem from a human-centered perspective helps us develop solutions that take emotions and a human perspective and create solutions based off of it.

Prototyping

Developing and iterating prototypes is integral to the design process. My preferred prototyping tool is Figma, where I've created low, mid, and high fidelity prototypes.

User Testing

Throughout my educational journey I have developed my skills when it comes to user testing. I have had years of expereinece conducting focus groups, developed surveys, and managed user interviews.
